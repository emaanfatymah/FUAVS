Face Tracking

Utilizing advanced AI algorithms, the Face Detection and Tracking module ensures the drone can lock onto and follow a person's face with high accuracy. This feature is ideal for personalized videography, security, and surveillance applications, as it guarantees the subject remains in focus and centered in the frame, enhancing the quality and effectiveness of the captured footage.

Body Tracking

The Body Detection and Tracking leverages sophisticated machine learning techniques to recognize and follow the movements of a person. This hands-free operation is particularly useful for activities like sports, outdoor events, and professional filming, where continuous and dynamic tracking is required. It ensures the drone can autonomously follow the subject, providing a seamless recording experience.

Hand Gesture Control

With the Hand Gesture Control, users can command the drone through simple and intuitive hand gestures. This feature eliminates the need for traditional remote controls, making drone operation accessible and engaging. It is especially beneficial in situations where quick, hands-free control is necessary, such as during active filming or in search and rescue missions.

Keypad Control

Keypad Control allows users to maneuver the drone with precision using a straightforward interface. Commands such as takeoff, land, move up/down, left/right, yaw, and perform flips are easily accessible, ensuring efficient and responsive control. This feature is perfect for both novice users and experienced pilots, providing a reliable and intuitive way to navigate the drone.

Fire Detection

The Fire Detection module provides advanced fire hazard identification, leveraging thermal imaging and AI algorithms. This feature is essential for early warning systems in areas prone to wildfires, industrial sites, and large public venues. It enhances safety by detecting fires early and enabling prompt response, potentially saving lives and property.

Gun Detection

To enhance the security capabilities of FUAVS by identifying firearms in real-time. This feature is crucial for surveillance in sensitive areas, such as schools, public events, and restricted zones. By promptly detecting potential threats, it ensures a rapid response, thereby increasing safety and security.
